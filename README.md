## 👋 About Me

I'm a **Data Analyst and full-stack data developer** with an active **TS/SCI clearance**, currently building data-driven applications at **IronEagleX** in Tampa, FL. My path here is anything but typical — I started as a **KC-130J aircraft mechanic and Collateral Duty Inspector** in the Marine Corps, where precision and accountability weren't optional. I carried that same mindset into the data world.

I bridge the gap between raw data and decision-making — from building production APIs in FastAPI to shipping interactive dashboards in Streamlit, managing PostgreSQL databases, and wiring it all together with Docker. I'm a builder who also knows how to brief the room.

> *"Mission-critical discipline applied to data engineering."*
